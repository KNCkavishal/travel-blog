My Sri Lanka Travel Stories

Sigiriya

Sigiriya, the ancient rock fortress, is one of Sri Lanka's most iconic landmarks and a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Highlights

  • Climb the rock fortress
  • Explore frescoes and gardens
  • Enjoy panoramic views

Nuwara Eliya

The "Little England" of Sri Lanka, known for its lush tea plantations, cool climate, and scenic landscapes.

Highlights

  • Visit tea factories
  • Gregory Lake boating
  • Walk around Victoria Park

Yala National Park

Yala is Sri Lanka's most famous wildlife park, home to leopards, elephants, and a wide variety of birds.

Highlights

  • Jeep safari to spot leopards
  • Birdwatching
  • Photograph elephants and wild animals
